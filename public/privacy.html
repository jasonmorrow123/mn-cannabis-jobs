Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

MN Cannabis Jobs ("we," "us," or "our") operates mncannabisjobs.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Job seekers: Email address when you sign up for job alerts
  • Employers: Company name, contact name, email address, phone number, and job posting details when you submit a job listing

We do not collect browsing data, cookies, or any information beyond what you directly submit.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send job alert emails to subscribers who opt in
  • To process and publish employer job listings
  • To contact employers regarding their listing submission and payment
  • To respond to inquiries sent to our email address

Email Communications

If you sign up for job alerts, we will email you when new relevant positions are posted. You can unsubscribe at any time by emailing admin@mncannabisjobs.com with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Employer contact details submitted through our job posting form are used solely for processing your listing.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our site uses HTTPS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to employer websites and third-party job platforms (such as Indeed or Vangst). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at admin@mncannabisjobs.com.
